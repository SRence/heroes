MIGUEL MALVAR Y CARPIO





Miguel Malvar y Carpio was remembered in history as the last Filipino

general who surrendered to the Americans.

He was born in San Miguel, Sto. Tomas, Batangas on September 1, 1865.

His parents were Maxima Malvar and Tiburcia Carpio. Miguel was the eldest among three children.

Malvar came from a wealthy family. He could have studied in Manila but he had to help his parents manage their properties and workers.

He even had to stop schooling to concentrate on the family business.

He was only able to finish second year high school.

In 1885, he married Maula Maloles, a childhood friend.

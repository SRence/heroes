FRANCISCO BALTAZAR





More popularly known as Balagtas, he is considered the prince of Tagalog poets.

Born in Panginay, Bigaa, Bulacan, on April 2, 1788.

He wrote Florante at Laura, a masterpiece of local versification, upholding moral and social values;

it served as the basic foundation of the Philippine literature.

Died in Orion, Bataan, on February 20, 1862.

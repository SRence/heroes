JOSE MA. PANGANIBAN





Avenger of Filipino honor. Born in Mambulao, Camarines Norte, on February 1, 1863.

A good friend and co-worker of Rizal.

He was Bicolandia's greatest contribution to the historic campaign for reforms, more popularly called the Propaganda Movement.

He wrote articles for La Solidaridad, under the pen names Jomapa and J.M.P. Died in Barcelona, Spain, on August 19, 1890

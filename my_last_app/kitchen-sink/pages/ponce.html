MARIANO PONCE





Researcher, historian, bibliographer, propagandist, diplomat, physician, folklorist, and an outstanding reformist.

Born in Baliwag, Bulacan, on March 23, 1863.

While in Spain, he joined the propaganda movement and became one of the initiators of La Solidaridad,

later becoming its managing editor. Died in HongKong on May 23, 1918.

ARELLANO CAYETANO





Cayetano Arellano was the first Chief Justice of the Supreme Court.

He was born on March 2, 1847 in Udyong, Bataan to parents Servando Cayetano and Cristina Lanzon.

Cayetano was only 5 years old when he started going to Colegio de San Juan de Letran

although he worked for the school in exchange for free education.

Because of his natural talent, getting high grades did not come hard for him.

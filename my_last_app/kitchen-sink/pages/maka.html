FRANCISCO MAKABULOS





Leader of the revolt in Tarlac. Born in La Paz, Tarlac, on September 17, 1871.

He was the first patriot in Tarlac, who joined the Katipunan.

General Aguinaldo appointed him as one of the brigadier generals of the Central Luzon area.

He established the Central Executive Committee, which had a constitution called the Constitution of Makabulos.

Died in La Paz, Tarlac, on April 30, 1922.

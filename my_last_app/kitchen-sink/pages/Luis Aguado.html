LUIS AGUADO





Luis Aguado was the son of a captain in the Spanish Navy.

He would later become supply chief of the Spanish arsenal in Fort San Felipe in the town of Cavite (now Cavite City).

He was married to Felisa Osorio, sister of Francisco Osorio and oldest daughter of Antonio Osorio,

a Chinese-Filipino businessman reputed to be the richest man in Cavite at that time. Aguado's widow would later marry Daniel Tria Tirona.

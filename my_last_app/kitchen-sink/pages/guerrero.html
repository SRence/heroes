FERNANDO MA. GUERRERO





Musician, journalist, politician, and lawyer.

Considered the greatest lyric poet in Spanish.

Born in Ermita, Manila, on May 30, 1873.

When the revolution broke out, General Antonio Luna invited him to join the editorial staff of La Independencia,

the organ of the revolution. Thus, he fought for the Filipino cause with his pen. Died in Manila June 12, 1929.

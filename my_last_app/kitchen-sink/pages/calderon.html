FELIPE CALDERON





Felipe Calderon was born on April 4, 1868 in Santa Cruz de Malabon (now Tanza), Cavite.

His father was Don Jose Gonzales Calderon while his mother was Dona Mdnuela Roca. Both had Spanish blood.

Calderon studied in Ateneo Municipal.

Even though he belonged to a rich family, he was granted scholarships because of his exceptional talent.

He completed with high honors his Bachelor of Arts degree.

He also enrolled at the University of Santo Tomas where he finished his Licenciado en Leyes in 1893.

Then, he took an apprentice job in the law office of Don Cayetano Arellano.

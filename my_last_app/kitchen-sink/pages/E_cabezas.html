EUGENIO CABEZAS





Eugenio Cabezas (born 1855 in Santa Cruz, Manila) was a goldsmith who was a Freemason and Katipunero.

He was married to Luisa Antonio of Cavite by whom he had seven children.

He owned a jewellery and watch repair shop on Calle Real (now called Trece Martires Avenue) in Cavite

which was used by the Katipunan as a meeting place.

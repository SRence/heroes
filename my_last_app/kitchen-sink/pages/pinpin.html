TOMAS PINPIN





Some historians dub Tomas Pinpin, as the "Prince of Filipino Printers" or the "Patriarch of Filipino Printing."

He learned the art of printing about the end of 1608 in the Dominican-owned printing press in Abucay.

In Abucay, Father Blancas employed in 1609 young Pinpin as an apprentice at the printing shop and taught him the art of printing.

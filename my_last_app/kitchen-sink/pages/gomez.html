MARIANO GOMEZ





Gomez was born on August 2, 1799 in the suburb of Santa Cruz, Manila.

He was a Tornatras, one born of mixed native (Filipino), Chinese and Spanish ancestries.

His parents were Alejandro Francisco Gomez and Martina Custodia.

After studying in the Colegio de San Juan de Letran, he took theology in the University of Santo Tomas.

He was a student preparing for the priesthood in the Seminary of Manila.

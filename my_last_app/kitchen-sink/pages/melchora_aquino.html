MELCHORA AQUINO





Better known as Tandang Sora. Born in Banlat, Kalookan City, on January 6, 1812.

She helped the Katipuneros under the leadership of Andres Bonifacio by providing them food, shelter, and other material goods.

She is recognized as the Grand Woman of the revolution and the Mother of Balintawak. She died on March 12, 1919.

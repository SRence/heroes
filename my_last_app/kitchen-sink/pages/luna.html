JUAN LUNA





A genius of the brush and a patriot of the highest order.

Creator of the world-famous painting, SPOLARIUM, which was awarded the gold medal in the Exposicion Nacional de Bellas Artes in Madrid in 1884.

It is also known as the greatest painting of all times. Born in Badoc, Ilocos, Norte, on October 23, 1857.

He was closely associated with the Reform Movement, together with Rizal, Lopez-Jaena, Ponce, etc. He died in Hongkong on December 7, 1899.

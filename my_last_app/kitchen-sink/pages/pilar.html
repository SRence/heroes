MARCELO H. DEL PILAR





Political analyst of the Filipino colony in Spain.

Born in Kupang, Bulacan, Bulacan, on August 30, 1850.

In 1882, he founded the nationalistic newspaper, Diariong Tagalog.

In December 1889, he became the editor of La Solidaridad and became the moving spirit behind the propaganda movement. Died on July 4, 1896.
